# AWL Zambia Website Audit — Execution Plan

## Context
AWL Zambia (awlzambia.com) is an explosives storage, dangerous goods warehousing, and mining logistics company in Kitwe, Zambia wanting to pivot into consulting. They need a comprehensive website audit to inform redesign, messaging overhaul, and lead generation strategy.

## Research Completed
- Crawled all AWL pages: Homepage, About, Services (5 subpages), Contact, Portfolio
- Benchmarked competitors: BME (bme.co.za), G&H Transport (ghtransport.africa), Unitrans Africa, DGM Group, 4PL Africa
- Reviewed market landscape: explosives storage, mining logistics, dangerous goods consulting in Zambia

## Critical Findings (Pre-Audit)

### Showstopper Issues
1. **Services page has LOREM IPSUM placeholder text** — Generic template cards with "Click edit button to change this text" visible
2. **No contact form** — Only email/phone, no enquiry form or consultation booking
3. **Zero consulting content** — The pivot has no foundation whatsoever on current site
4. **No trust assets** — No client logos, case studies, testimonials, certifications displayed, team photos
5. **~5 real pages total** — Far too thin for authority or SEO

### Strengths Identified
- Real operational credibility (2013, explosives magazines, 60+ vehicles, 7 border offices)
- Platinum Compliance rating mentioned
- Genuine niche specialization (Class 1/5.1 dangerous goods, Copperbelt)
- Compelling founder story (Tammy Bruno, continuity through adversity)

## Deliverable Structure
The audit will be a single comprehensive document covering all 14 sections requested:

1. Executive Summary
2. Business Positioning Audit
3. Messaging & Copy Audit (with rewrites)
4. Website Structure / Information Architecture Audit (with new sitemap)
5. UX / UI / Credibility Audit (with scores)
6. CRO Audit (with CTA strategy)
7. SEO Audit (with keyword strategy)
8. Consulting Pivot Audit (with offer framework)
9. Trust, Compliance & Risk Communication Audit
10. Competitor / Market Standard Lens
11. Repositioning Strategy
12. New Sitemap & Page Blueprint
13. Priority Action Plan (30/60/90 day)
14. Final Strategic Recommendation + Boardroom Version + Bonus Sections

## Output
- Write full audit as a markdown document to `audits/awl-zambia-website-audit.md`
- Include all suggested rewrites, CTAs, sitemap, keyword lists, and action plan
- Estimated length: 8,000-12,000 words
