Our Services
Integrated Dangerous Goods & Mining Logistics
Every service delivered under our Platinum Compliance framework, with licensed facilities and trained personnel at every step.

Explosives Storage & Management

Licensed magazines approved by the Zambian Mines Safety Department for Class 1 Packaged Explosives and Initiating Systems. Earth-mound protected, 24/7 secured.

Dangerous Goods Warehousing

10,000m² of purpose-built warehousing for Class 5.1, 3, 6.1, and 8 dangerous goods. FIFO dispatch, weekly stock counts, full traceability.

Specialist Transport & Delivery

60+ GPS-tracked vehicles including emulsion tankers, inter-links, and tri-axle trailers. Every driver dangerous goods certified.

Customs Clearance & Forwarding

AWL Clearing operates at 7 major border crossings: Chirundu, Kasumbalesa, Vic Falls, Kazangula, Chipata, Katima Mulilo, Nakonde.

Sea Freight & Port Logistics

Containerised and break-bulk dangerous goods via Dar es Salaam, Mombasa, and Beira. GPS-tracked fleet with 30+ years of partner experience.
