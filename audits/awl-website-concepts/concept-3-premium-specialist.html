Our Heritage
A Legacy of Excellence & Resilience

Founded in 2013 by Tammy Bruno — a Zambian entrepreneur and hands-on logistics professional — AWL was established to address the critical need for dedicated dangerous goods storage in the Copperbelt.

From a single warehouse, AWL grew into an integrated logistics operation with 60+ vehicles, 5 explosives magazines, and clearing offices at 7 international border crossings.

"Following the loss of our founder in 2021, Managing Director Mekela Bastos continues the legacy — upholding the same standards of safety, service, and operational excellence that define AWL."

Today, AWL stands as Zambia's premier dangerous goods logistics specialist, now expanding into strategic advisory services to share 13 years of hard-won expertise with the industry.
