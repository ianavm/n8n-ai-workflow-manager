Our Story
Built on Expertise, Resilience & Trust
2013

Founded by Tammy Bruno

AWL established to address the critical need for dedicated Class 5.1 Ammonium Nitrate storage in Zambia's Copperbelt.

2015-2019

Growth & Expansion

Fleet expanded to 60+ vehicles. Customs clearing offices opened at 7 major border crossings across Southern-Central Africa.

2020

Explosives Magazines Acquired

Licensed explosives magazines added — expanding capability into Class 1 Packaged Explosives and Initiating Systems storage.

2021

Leadership Transition

Following the loss of founder Tammy Bruno, Mekela Bastos assumes the role of Managing Director, continuing the legacy.

Present

Operations & Advisory

AWL expands into strategic consulting — making 13 years of operational expertise available to companies across the sector.
