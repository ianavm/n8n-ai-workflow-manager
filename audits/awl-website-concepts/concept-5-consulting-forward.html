Our Services
Dual-Capability: Operations & Advisory
Two sides of the same expertise. Operations proves our capability. Advisory shares our knowledge.

Explosives Storage & Management

Licensed magazines for Class 1 Packaged Explosives and Initiating Systems. Earth-mound protected, 24/7 secured, daily reconciliation.

Dangerous Goods Warehousing

10,000m² of purpose-built capacity for Class 5.1, 3, 6.1, and 8 goods. FIFO, weekly counts, full traceability.

Specialist Transport

60+ GPS-tracked vehicles including emulsion tankers, inter-links, tri-axles. Every driver DG-certified.

Customs Clearance

7 border offices: Chirundu, Kasumbalesa, Vic Falls, Kazangula, Chipata, Katima Mulilo, Nakonde.

Sea Freight & Ports

Containerised and break-bulk DG via Dar es Salaam, Mombasa, and Beira. 30+ year partner network.

Compliance & Regulatory Readiness

Explosives licensing guidance, SHE systems design, regulatory gap analysis, and audit preparation. Guided by our Platinum Compliance experience.

Storage & Facility Consulting

Magazine design, warehouse planning, security infrastructure, and expansion strategy. We've built ours — we'll help you build yours.

Mining Supply Chain Advisory

End-to-end supply chain assessment, optimisation, vendor management, and cross-border logistics strategy.

Zambia Market Entry

For companies entering the Zambian mining and explosives sector. Regulatory navigation, local partners, operations setup.

Outsourced Operations

Don't want to build your own? Use ours. Dedicated storage, managed logistics, contracted fleet, full compliance.
