## Key Elements in Every Email

### ✅ What Makes These Work:

1. **Industry-specific pain point** in subject (not generic)
2. **Concrete data** (percentages, time saved, revenue gained)
3. **Local social proof** ("A [similar business] in [nearby area]...")
4. **One clear focus** (not a laundry list of services)
5. **Low-commitment CTA** (15-min call, not "book a demo")
6. **Professional tone** (consultant, not salesperson)

### ❌ What's Avoided:

- Generic openers ("I hope this finds you well")
- Buzzwords ("synergy", "leverage", "cutting-edge")
- Multiple CTAs or confused value props
- Pushy language or urgency tactics
- Long paragraphs or complex explanations

## Email Personalization Factors

The AI generates unique emails based on:

1. **Business name** (personalized greeting)
2. **Industry type** (specific pain points and automation opportunities)
3. **Location** (Fourways/Johannesburg social proof)
4. **Website analysis** (if complex site = likely larger business = different messaging)
5. **Lead score** (high-fit = more aggressive CTA, low-fit = softer approach)

## Response Handling

When leads reply, they go to: **ian@anyvisionmedia.com**

**Positive responses** to look for:
- "Tell me more"
- "What does this cost?"
- "When can we chat?"
- "Send me more info"
- "Book me in"

**Neutral responses** (follow up):
- "Not right now but interested"
- "Call me in [timeframe]"
- "Send me your website"

**Negative responses** (remove from list):
- "Unsubscribe"
- "Not interested"
- "Remove me"

The workflow automatically updates Airtable when emails are sent, so you can track:
- Who got an email (Status: "Email Sent")
- When it was sent (Email Sent Date)
- Their automation fit score (prioritize high-fit for follow-up)

## Testing Recommendation

Before the first Monday run, do a manual test:

1. Change `maxResults` to `5` in Search Config
2. Run manually ("Test workflow" button)
3. Check the 5 emails that get generated
4. Verify they:
   - Have compelling subjects
   - Show industry knowledge
   - Include concrete ROI data
   - Feel consultative, not salesy

If any emails feel generic or off-brand, we can tune the AI prompt further.
