# Vol. 2 Demo Pack — Short-Form Content Scripts

Nine recordable scripts, one per workflow. Every script is engineered to
retain past the 3-second scroll cutoff, deliver one visceral result, and
drive a DM or link click without sounding like a guru.

**Brand voice (Ian / AnyVision Media):** direct, dry, anti-hype,
South African context (ZAR, SA businesses), never "AI gurus hate this",
never "You won't believe...". The confidence comes from the demo, not
the adjectives.

---

## Master principles (read once, apply to every script)

### The 6-part short-form skeleton (2026)

| Beat | Length | Job |
|---|---|---|
| **Pattern interrupt** | 0-2s | Break the scroll — visual change + text overlay with the *claim* |
| **Stake-setting** | 2-6s | Name the painful before-state in concrete, specific language |
| **The reveal** | 6-15s | The "watch" moment — demo the workflow firing |
| **Proof visible** | 15-30s | The result on screen (inbox ping / Sheet row / Slack alert) |
| **Tighten the loop** | 30-45s | State the delta ("2 hours of admin, done in 20 seconds") |
| **Soft CTA** | 45-60s | Low-friction ask — "comment 'workflow' and I'll DM you" or "link in bio" |

**Targets:** TikTok / IG Reels 45-60s, YouTube Shorts 60-75s, LinkedIn
video 60-90s. Cap at 90s for every platform — longer drops retention.

### Hook formulas that still work in 2026

1. **The negation hook** — "Stop doing X. Do Y instead." (Tension, strong POV.)
2. **The timer-on-screen** — stopwatch overlay. Brain can't not watch.
3. **Concrete-number opener** — "47 hours. That's the average reply time for a website lead."
4. **Before/After split-screen** — left = pain, right = result. Zero narration needed.
5. **Callout hook** — "If you've ever said 'I meant to follow up with X'..."
6. **Contrarian thesis** — "Everyone's overcomplicating AI. It's actually this simple."
7. **The receipt** — screenshot of the inbox / Sheet / result *first*, then explain.
8. **Live-build** — blank canvas, stopwatch, build the thing on camera. Inherently retentive.
9. **The reveal loop** — ask a question in frame 1, withhold the answer until frame 30.
10. **The "this is the one" framing** — "Out of the 40 workflows I've built this year, this is the one I show clients first."

### Retention mechanics

- **Caption-first viewers.** 60%+ watch with sound off. On-screen text carries the story.
- **Visual churn every 3-5s.** Cut, zoom, highlight, overlay. Static kills.
- **Subtitle every spoken word** with CapCut / Submagic / Opus auto-captions — bold brand colour on keywords.
- **Open loop in frame 1, close in frame N.** "You won't believe what's in row 3" is dead; "Watch what happens to the Sheet" works because you *actually show the Sheet*.
- **No logo bug.** Brand lives in consistent font + colour, not a watermark.

### CTA patterns that convert without tanking reach

- **Soft DM bait:** "I'll DM the template to the first 20 people who comment 'workflow'." Works on TikTok + IG.
- **Link-in-bio nudge:** one frame at the end with "link in bio → workflow templates". Safest on IG.
- **LinkedIn variant:** put the CTA in the caption, not the video. "Full workflow in the comments." Drives comments (which boost LI reach) without gating.
- **Never:** "Like and subscribe", "hit that bell", "share with someone who..."

### Platform-specific deltas (high-confidence)

| Platform | Aspect | Length sweet spot | First-frame text | Caption length |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **TikTok** | 9:16 | 45-60s | Huge, top-third, 2 lines max | Short — pattern interrupt |
| **IG Reels** | 9:16 | 30-50s | Centred, bold | Punchy + 5 niche hashtags |
| **YT Shorts** | 9:16 | 60-75s | Similar to TikTok | Detailed — SEO-adjacent |
| **LinkedIn** | 4:5 or 1:1 | 60-90s | Top-left, smaller | Long — 3 paragraphs, story-led |

### The AVM brand rules

- Font: Inter Tight for text overlays (matches landing page brand).
- Colour: #FF6D5A (brand orange) for keyword highlights; charcoal for body.
- Never say "artificial intelligence" in full — always "AI".
- Always use SA Rand (R) for numbers, never USD.
- Humour: dry observation, not punchlines. "I don't love admin. I also don't love losing leads. I built this." > "This workflow SLAPS".
- Founder-first POV. "I built X." > "We built X." Agency-but-solo framing.

### Posting cadence (recommended)

- **Mon 07:00 SAST:** LinkedIn video (B2B buyers on lunch break)
- **Mon 18:00 SAST:** TikTok (SA peak + global Europe evening)
- **Wed 12:00 SAST:** IG Reels cross-post from TikTok
- **Thu 07:00 SAST:** YT Shorts (SEO tail + discovery)
- **Sat 10:00 SAST:** Second TikTok — testing hooks from the best-performing video

## Testing framework

For every video, log these 5 signals:

| Signal | Where to check | Fail threshold |
|---|---|---|
| **3s retention** | Platform analytics | <60% = kill the hook, reshoot |
| **Average watch time** | Platform analytics | <40% of video length = recut |
| **Saves** | IG / TikTok | Target >5% of views for educational content |
| **DM count** | Instagram DMs / TikTok inbox | Target 10+ per 10k views for demo content |
| **Comment ratio** | Platform analytics | Target 0.5%+ comments per view — LI especially |

If the first 5 seconds bleed viewers, test a new hook variant from the list. If the whole video keeps watch time below 30%, the problem is the reveal — not the hook.
