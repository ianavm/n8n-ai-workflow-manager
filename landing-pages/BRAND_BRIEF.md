# AnyVision Media — Brand Brief

## Company
- **Name**: AnyVision Media
- **Tagline**: "AI-Powered Transformation for Your Business"
- **Industry**: AI Consulting & Integration
- **What we do**: We help businesses integrate artificial intelligence into their workflows, products, and operations. From strategy to deployment — we handle it all.

## Services
1. **AI Strategy Consulting** — Roadmaps, assessments, and AI readiness audits
2. **Custom AI Solutions** — Bespoke models, chatbots, automation pipelines
3. **AI Integration** — Connecting AI into existing tech stacks (CRM, ERP, workflows)
4. **Computer Vision** — Object detection, image analysis, video intelligence
5. **NLP & LLM Solutions** — Custom GPT apps, document processing, semantic search
6. **AI Training & Workshops** — Upskilling teams on AI tools and best practices

## Target Audience
- CTOs, VP Engineering, Heads of Innovation at mid-market and enterprise companies
- Business owners looking to automate and scale with AI
- Tech teams that need AI expertise they don't have in-house

## Brand Personality
- **Expert but approachable** — We know AI deeply, but we explain it simply
- **Forward-thinking** — Always on the cutting edge
- **Results-driven** — We focus on ROI and business outcomes, not buzzwords
- **Trustworthy** — Enterprise-grade security and reliability

## Visual Identity
- **Primary Color**: #0A0F1C (deep navy/near-black)
- **Accent Color**: #6C63FF (electric violet/purple)
- **Secondary Accent**: #00D4AA (teal/mint green)
- **Gradient**: #6C63FF → #00D4AA (purple to teal)
- **Text on dark**: #FFFFFF and #B0B8C8 (muted silver)
- **Typography**: Clean, modern sans-serif (Inter, DM Sans, or similar)
- **Style**: Premium, tech-forward, clean with purposeful animations

## Landing Page Requirements
Every variant MUST include:
1. **Hero section** — Headline, subheadline, CTA button, visual element
2. **Social proof** — Client logos, stats, or testimonials (use realistic placeholders)
3. **Services section** — The 6 services listed above
4. **How it works** — 3-4 step process
5. **Case studies / results** — 2-3 example outcomes with metrics
6. **Team / About** — Brief company story
7. **CTA section** — Book a consultation / contact form
8. **Footer** — Links, socials, copyright
9. **Navigation** — Sticky/fixed top nav with smooth scroll
10. **Mobile responsive** — Must work on all screen sizes
11. **Accessibility** — WCAG 2.1 AA compliant
