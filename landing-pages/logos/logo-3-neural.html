DESIGN NOTES
Concept: 8 nodes arranged as a neural constellation forming an abstract letter "A"
Hierarchy: Apex node (teal, largest) + central hub (gradient) + 4 primary (violet) + 2 base (faded)
Connections: 11 edges with varying opacity creating depth and visual hierarchy
Glow halos: Apex and central hub have subtle outer rings for emphasis
Light mode: Slightly deeper violet (#5A52E0) and teal (#00B894) for contrast on white
Small sizes: Thicker strokes and larger nodes at 24-32px for legibility
