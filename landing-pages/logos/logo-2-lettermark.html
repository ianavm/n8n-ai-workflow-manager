Design Rationale
Connected Letterform

The A, V, and M share a continuous connected silhouette. The three letters flow as a single zigzag form -- peak (A), valley (V), double-peak (M) -- creating visual unity while each letter remains individually legible. Shared strokes between adjacent letters reduce visual clutter.

Central V Prominence

The V occupies the center of the composition, naturally formed by the descending inner strokes meeting at the valley point. The negative space between the A and M inner legs reinforces the V shape, creating a subtle checkmark/validation reference appropriate for AI verification.

A Crossbar via Even-Odd

The A's horizontal crossbar is cut from the filled shape using SVG fill-rule="evenodd", creating a true negative-space detail that works on any background color without needing a background-matched fill. This ensures the logo is truly background-agnostic.

Gradient Direction

The 135-degree gradient flows from electric violet (#6C63FF) at the A to teal/mint (#00D4AA) at the M, creating a natural left-to-right reading direction and reinforcing the sense of forward motion, transformation, and technological progression.
