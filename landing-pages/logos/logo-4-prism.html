Design Notes

Prism Concept

The upward-pointing triangular prism symbolizes AnyVision's ability to see through multiple perspectives. Light enters and refracts into the brand gradient, representing the transformation AI brings to every challenge.

V-Notch Detail

The "V" cutout at the base of the triangle serves double duty: it embeds "Vision" into the mark itself, and creates the visual separation between the two faceted faces of the prism, enhancing the 3D quality at any size.

Light Refraction

Subtle light beams emerge from the right side of the prism at larger sizes, creating the signature "refraction" effect. These beams fade gracefully at smaller sizes, maintaining clarity while adding depth when space allows.

Scalability

The bold triangular silhouette remains recognizable down to 16px favicon size. The two-tone face split (violet left, teal right) provides instant brand recognition even without the refraction details at small scales.
