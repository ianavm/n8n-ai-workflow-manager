Icon Mark

The mark is an abstract geometric eye built from concentric arc layers. The outer form uses two sweeping bezier curves creating a pointed vesica piscis (lens). Inside, a faceted diamond iris contains a negative-space "V" formed by the gap between two triangular facets. A circular pupil with a specular highlight anchors the center.

Design Rationale

The Eye Form — Two quadratic bezier curves create a vesica piscis (pointed lens shape). This geometric form has been associated with vision, insight, and perception across cultures for millennia. The pointed tips left and right give the mark a sense of alert, directed focus.

Layered Depth — Three concentric layers build visual richness: a subtle semi-transparent fill establishes the eye's mass, a bold gradient stroke defines its edge, and an inner iris ring provides structure. This layering creates depth even at small sizes and gives the mark a premium, dimensional quality.

The Faceted Iris — Four triangular facets form a diamond shape, split by a horizontal gap through the center. Reading top-to-bottom, the upper pair of triangles converge inward (forming an "A" shape), while the lower pair diverge outward (forming a "V"). This subtly embeds both letters of "AV" (AnyVision) into the geometric construction without being literal.

The Gradient Flow — Electric violet (#6C63FF) to teal mint (#00D4AA) flows diagonally across the icon. Violet signals creativity, innovation, and premium technology; teal conveys trust, precision, and forward-thinking. The 135-degree angle creates natural top-left to bottom-right movement.

The Specular Highlight — A single white circle offset to the upper-left of the pupil brings the eye to life with a photorealistic glint. At small sizes, it merges into the surrounding geometry without affecting legibility.

Dark / Light Adaptation — The dark variant uses brighter gradient stops (#6C63FF / #00D4AA) against the deep navy (#0A0F1C) pupil. The light variant uses slightly darkened stops (#5A52E0 / #00B892) to maintain WCAG contrast on white, with a white pupil that matches the background.

Typography — "Any" in neutral white/dark anchors the wordmark, while "Vision" in the horizontal gradient draws the eye and reinforces the brand concept. "MEDIA" in lightweight, widely-spaced uppercase beneath provides a corporate subtitle without competing with the primary mark.
