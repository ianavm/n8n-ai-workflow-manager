Design Anatomy

The orbital icon deconstructed: three tilted ellipses at -30deg, +30deg, and 90deg create the atom/orbit structure. A radial-gradient glow sits behind the white core node.

Ring 1: violet-to-teal
Ring 2: teal-to-violet (reversed)
Ring 3: vertical orbit (subtle)
Core: glow + gradient + white
