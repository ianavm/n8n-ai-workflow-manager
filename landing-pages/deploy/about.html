The Story

Building South Africa's First AI Company

I'm Ian Immelman — and I'm documenting every step of building an AI automation empire from Johannesburg.

II Photo coming soon

Ian Immelman

Founder & AI Engineer
Johannesburg, South Africa

I started AnyVision Media with a simple belief: every business in South Africa deserves access to the same AI automation that Fortune 500 companies use.

Before AnyVision, I spent years watching businesses drown in manual work — copying data between spreadsheets, chasing invoices by hand, posting content one platform at a time. I knew AI could fix all of it.

So I built it. Not just for one business, but as a platform that any company can plug into.

Today, AnyVision Media runs 80+ autonomous workflows, 22 AI agents, and serves businesses across 7 departments — from accounting to marketing to lead generation. Everything is automated. Everything is intelligent.

Now I'm on a mission to make AnyVision Media South Africa's first million-dollar AI company. And I'm documenting the entire journey — the wins, the failures, and everything in between.
