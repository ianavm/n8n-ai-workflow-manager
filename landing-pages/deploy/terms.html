7. Overage Billing

Each Plan includes specified usage allowances for messages and leads. Usage that exceeds your Plan limits will incur overage charges as follows:

  • Messages: R0.50 per message above your Plan limit.
  • Leads: R2.00 per lead above your Plan limit.

Hard Cap Protection

To prevent runaway costs, a hard cap is enforced at 2x (two times) your Plan's usage limit. Once the hard cap is reached, delivery of messages and lead processing will pause until the start of the next billing period, unless you upgrade to a higher Plan.

Usage Alerts

Automated alerts will be sent to your registered email address when your usage reaches 80% and 95% of your Plan limits, giving you adequate notice to manage your usage or upgrade your Plan.

Overage Invoicing

Overage charges are calculated and billed at the end of each billing period, separate from your base subscription fee.
