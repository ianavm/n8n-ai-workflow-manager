General

AnyVision Media is an AI consulting company based in Johannesburg, South Africa. We build and deploy AI-powered automation systems that handle repetitive business tasks — from invoicing and lead generation to SEO, content production, and customer support.
AI agents are autonomous software programs that handle specific business processes. Each agent monitors data, makes decisions, and executes actions — like sending follow-up emails, reconciling payments, or optimising ad campaigns — without manual intervention.
Sign up and choose a plan. We schedule a guided onboarding call (included on Growth and Enterprise plans, optional add-on for others). We configure your departments and activate your workflows within 48 hours. You get portal access from day one.
Any industry with repetitive workflows: professional services, real estate, e-commerce, legal, accounting firms, marketing agencies, healthcare, and more. Our platform is industry-agnostic — the AI adapts to your specific processes.
Johannesburg, Gauteng, South Africa. We serve clients nationwide across South Africa and internationally via our cloud-based platform.

Four tiers: Lite (R1,999/mo), Starter (R5,999/mo), Growth (R14,999/mo), and Enterprise (R29,999/mo). Annual billing saves approximately 17% (2 months free). International pricing available in USD, EUR, and GBP.

South African clients: PayFast (credit card, debit card, EFT, SnapScan). International clients: Stripe (credit and debit cards in USD, EUR, or GBP).
ZAR prices exclude 15% VAT, which is added at checkout. International prices (USD, EUR, GBP) are not subject to South African VAT.
